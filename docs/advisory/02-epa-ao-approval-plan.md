# EPA — Awarding Organisation Approval Plan

**Phase 1 Critical Path (July – Dec 2026)**
**Objective:** Secure Awarding Organisation (AO) recognition in time to certify the first cohort at the Birmingham launch, **January 2027**.

Without AO approval no qualification can be certified, so the Birmingham launch is blocked. This plan reverse-engineers from Jan 2027 and treats AO approval as the single load-bearing dependency for Phase 1.

---

## 1. Target Awarding Organisation

Recommended shortlist for an EV / electrical-connections technical qualification:

| AO | Fit | Lead time | Fee band | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Highfield Qualifications** | Strong on vocational / technical | ~12 weeks | ££ | Fast approval, good centre support |
| **NOCN** | Broad construction/utilities scope | ~16 weeks | ££ | Recognised for CSCS-adjacent qualifications |
| **City & Guilds** | Blue-chip brand, electrical heritage | ~20–26 weeks | £££ | Slower, but highest market recognition |
| **Innovate Awarding** | Employer-led, flexible units | ~12 weeks | ££ | Good for bespoke qualifications |

**Recommendation:** Highfield as primary, City & Guilds as strategic parallel track if brand value justifies the timeline risk. Final call needed from Andrew / Callum by **end July 2026**.
